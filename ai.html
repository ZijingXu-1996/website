AI in the ECCSR Framework

Artificial Intelligence is transforming project management by enhancing decision-making, automating routine tasks, and providing predictive insights. When aligned with ECCSR principles, AI can support ethical decision-making, improve collaboration, ensure sustainable practices, and deliver measurable results.

Ethical Considerations for AI in Projects

Data Privacy

Ensure compliance with GDPR and other regulations when collecting and processing project data.

Algorithmic Bias

Regularly audit AI systems for unintended bias in decision-making recommendations.

Human Oversight

Maintain human control over critical project decisions, using AI as advisory support.

Transparency

Be clear with stakeholders about where and how AI is being used in the project.
