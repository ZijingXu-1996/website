The Ethical Dimension of ECCSR

Ethics is fundamental to the ECCSR framework, reminding us that every project decision has ethical implications. Ethical project management goes beyond legal compliance to consider how our actions impact all stakeholders. This is particularly important for small businesses or charities where trust and reputation are paramount.

E

Ethical Considerations

Ethics is the foundation of project sustainability, especially in today's environment of corporate social responsibility. The ECCSR framework emphasizes that project decisions must be ethical and avoid harming the interests of stakeholders. It is recommended that companies establish an ethical checklist covering data privacy, fair pay and environmental impact. At the same time, anonymous reporting channels should be set up to encourage employees and volunteers to raise ethical concerns.

Ethical Decision-Making Framework

When facing ethical dilemmas in your projects, consider this practical 4-step approach:

1

Identify the Issue

Clearly define the ethical concern. Who is affected? What values are in conflict?

2

Evaluate Alternatives

Consider all possible actions and their potential consequences using ethical principles.

3

Make a Decision

Choose the option that best aligns with ethical standards and organizational values.

4

Implement & Reflect

Act on your decision and evaluate the outcomes for future learning.

Ethical Project Checklist

  • Have we considered all stakeholders in our decisions?
  • Are we being transparent about project limitations and risks?
  • Are team members treated fairly and with respect?
  • Does this project create value without causing harm?
  • Would we be comfortable if our decisions were publicly known?

Case Study: Cork Manufacturing Firm

A family-owned business faced pressure to cut costs by using cheaper, non-recyclable materials. After applying ethical analysis, they instead:

  • Invested in sustainable materials despite higher initial costs
  • Communicated the environmental benefits to customers
  • Trained staff in sustainable practices

This decision led to a 20% increase in sales from environmentally-conscious customers within 9 months.
