Planning in the ECCSR Framework

Careful project planning can effectively reduce uncertainty. This guide recommends a combination of agile and traditional methods. Make a milestone plan to clarify key nodes, and reserve redundant time to buffer against unexpected situations. Small teams can use a two-week sprint cycle to maintain flexibility.

P

Planning Principles

While not explicitly in the ECCSR acronym, planning is the bridge that connects all framework elements. It translates ethical considerations into action plans, structures collaboration, incorporates sustainability measures, and defines the path to achieving results.

Essential Planning Components

Clearly define what is included in the project (and what isn't) to prevent scope creep. Document deliverables, boundaries, and constraints.

  • Project charter
  • Work breakdown structure
  • Scope statement

Create realistic timelines that account for dependencies and resource availability.

  • Gantt charts
  • Milestone planning
  • Critical path analysis

Estimate costs accurately and plan for contingencies.

  • Cost breakdown structure
  • Cash flow projections
  • Funding strategies

Assign the right people and materials to tasks based on availability and skills.

  • Resource calendars
  • Skills matrix
  • Procurement planning

Planning Timeline for Small Projects

Week 1-2

Initiation & Scoping

Define project objectives, scope, and success criteria

Week 3

Detailed Planning

Develop work breakdown structure and initial schedule

Week 4

Resource Planning

Assign team members and secure necessary materials

Week 5

Final Plan Review

Validate plan with stakeholders and obtain approvals

Case Study: Galway Retail Startup

A boutique clothing store improved their launch project through better planning:

  • Detailed 12-week countdown checklist
  • Visual planning board in the stockroom
  • Weekly planning meetings with clear action items
  • Contingency budget for unexpected delays

This planning rigor helped them open on schedule despite supplier issues, achieving 90% of first-month sales targets.
